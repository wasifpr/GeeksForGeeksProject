Supplier Development Agreement & Statement of Work

Document ID: SOW-2026-APEX-01  |  Version: 1.0  |  Date: June 21, 2026

1. Parties and Scope

This Agreement is entered into by and between Apex Software Solutions ("Supplier") and the Product Team ("Client"). Under this Statement of Work (SOW), the Supplier will provide software development services to design, build, and deploy the Document Collaboration Module V2.1 as described in the technical specifications.

2. Services to be Delivered

  • Frontend UI Development: Implementation of all user interface components matching client-approved Figma wireframes, ensuring responsive layout and accessibility.
  • Backend API Integration: Core server-side coding utilizing the section-based locking synchronizer to support collaborative block editing.
  • Redis Caching Layer: Setup and integration of a single-instance Redis cache to support performance requirements.
  • Staging & Quality Assurance Support: Deployment to a staging environment and active participation in bug resolution during the Client's manual testing phase.
  • Production Launch Support: Launch monitoring, including 10 hours of post-go-live standby support.
Note on Out-of-Scope Work: Any requests outside the defined deliverables (such as multi-region DB replication or third-party CRM integrations) will be treated as ad-hoc services and billed at the standard rate of $75/hour, subject to written change orders.

3. Payment Terms and Milestones

Payment shall be structured around the following project milestones, invoiced upon completion and sign-off:

  • Milestone 1: Down Payment – 20% ($10,000) due upon execution of this contract to initiate design and architectural setup.
  • Milestone 2: Frontend Delivery – 30% ($15,000) due upon deployment of the mock UI components to the developer environment and client visual approval.
  • Milestone 3: API & Redis Integration – 30% ($15,000) due upon successful deployment to staging and verification of the 2-second synchronization window.
  • Milestone 4: Final Sign-off – 20% ($10,000) due within 30 days of production launch and client final acceptance.

Standard payment terms are Net-30 from the date of invoice receipt. Ad-hoc hourly services will be invoiced monthly on a Net-15 basis.

4. Performance & Environment SLA

  • Staging Environment: Supplier guarantees 99.0% uptime for the developer staging environment during the verification phase, excluding scheduled maintenance.
  • Warranty Period: Critical bugs (severity 1 or 2) reported during the 30-day post-launch acceptance window will be patched within 48 hours at no additional charge.
