SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) - ENTERPRISE PLAN

1. Monthly Uptime Guarantee:
Service provider guarantees a Monthly Uptime Percentage of 99.99% for all production API endpoints. 

2. Service Credit Schedule:
In the event that the service provider fails to meet the Monthly Uptime Guarantee, the customer is eligible to request service credits according to the following bands:
- Monthly Uptime 99.90% to 99.98%: 10% credit of the monthly fee.
- Monthly Uptime 99.50% to 99.89%: 25% credit of the monthly fee.
- Monthly Uptime under 99.50%: 50% credit of the monthly fee.

3. Support Escalation Targets:
- Critical Outage (P0 blocker): Initial response target is 30 minutes.
- High Degradation (P1 degradation): Initial response target is 2 hours.
- Support agents are authorized to credit up to $500 manually; higher amounts require Product Owner approval.
