SUPPLIER DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT & STATEMENT OF WORK V1.0

1. Parties and Scope:
This agreement is between Apex Software Solutions ("Supplier") and the Product Team ("Client"). The Supplier will provide software development services for the Document Collaboration Module V2.1.

2. Services to be Delivered:
- Frontend UI development matching client-approved Figma wireframes.
- Backend API integration using section-based locking synchronizer.
- Integration of a single-instance Redis cache.
- Deployment to staging environment and support during manual QA.
- Production launch support (10 hours post-go-live included).

3. Payment Terms and Milestones:
- Milestone 1: Down Payment (20% of total fee, $10,000) due upon signing of this contract.
- Milestone 2: Frontend Delivery (30% of total fee, $15,000) due upon demo and visual approval.
- Milestone 3: API & Redis Integration (30% of total fee, $15,000) due upon successful deployment to staging and verification of the 2-second synchronization.
- Milestone 4: Final Sign-off (20% of total fee, $10,000) due within 30 days of production launch and client acceptance.
- Additional Ad-hoc Services: Billed at $75 per hour, invoiced monthly with Net-15 terms.

4. Performance & Environment SLA:
- Supplier guarantees 99.0% uptime for the developer staging environment during the testing phase.
- Critical bugs reported during the 30-day post-launch acceptance window will be patched within 48 hours at no additional cost.
- Standard payment terms: Net-30 from date of invoice receipt.
